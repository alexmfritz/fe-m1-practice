The MCU Timeline

With Marvel Studios recently expanding it's properties into canonical television shows like: WandaVision and Loki, it's hard to keep track of the correct order to watch all of the Marvel media.

So how do you watch the MCU in the chronological narrative order?

As you can see from above, the Marvel Cinematic Universe has now expanded into a Multiverse of endless possibilities. Recent properties such as Loki and What If...?, along with the upcoming Spider-man: No Way Home will be dealing with the rising implications of powerful beings breaching through the barriers of the infinite universes and causing mayhem in places they do not originate from. It's very timey-whimey and confusing, but truly showcases the expansive planning Kevin Feige and his team at Marvel Studios have done.
